About GameHelper

01

History of GameHelper creation

GameHelper was born from a simple frustration — spending hours browsing steam reviews and youtube videos just to find a game worth playing on a Friday night. We believed there had to be a smarter way.

In early 2026, the project kicked off as a personal experiment: could a few smart questions replace hours of research? The answer turned out to be yes. The algorithm learned to match player personalities to game experiences with surprising accuracy, and GameHelper went from a weekend project to a fully-fledged recommendation platform.

Today GameHelper helps players of all experience levels — from casual weekend gamers to dedicated enthusiasts — discover titles they genuinely love. Every recommendation is powered by thoughtful questions about playstyle, mood, and preferences.
